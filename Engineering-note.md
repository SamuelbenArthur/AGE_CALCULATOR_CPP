# Engineering Note

## Topic

Age Calculation Using Date Arithmetic

## Formula Used

Age = Current Date − Birth Date

The calculation is performed by comparing:

* Years
* Months
* Days

When the current day is smaller than the birth day, days are borrowed from the previous month. When the current month is smaller than the birth month, months are borrowed from the previous year.

## Meaning of Variables

* Birth Day = Day of birth
* Birth Month = Month of birth
* Birth Year = Year of birth
* Current Day = Present day
* Current Month = Present month
* Current Year = Present year

## Why This Matters in Engineering

Date calculations are widely used in engineering and technology. Engineers use date calculations to:

* Schedule maintenance activities.
* Determine equipment service intervals.
* Track project timelines.
* Calculate product warranty periods.
* Monitor inspection and calibration dates.

Accurate date calculations help engineers improve planning, maintenance, and system reliability.

## Sample Input

Birth Date

Day = 15

Month = 8

Year = 2002

Current Date

Day = 10

Month = 6

Year = 2026

## Sample Output

Age = 23 Years, 9 Months, 26 Days
